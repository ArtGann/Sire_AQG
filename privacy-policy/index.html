Privacy Policy

How All Quality Gutters LLC collects, uses, protects, and handles information submitted through this website and related communications.

Effective Date: June 30, 2026

All Quality Gutters LLC ("Company," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you provide when you visit our website, request an estimate, contact us, communicate with us by phone, email, SMS, or use any of our services.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what choices you have regarding your personal information.

By using our website or submitting your information through our forms, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out a form, request a free estimate, contact us, schedule a service, upload photos, or communicate with our team.

The information we may collect includes:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Property address
  • City, state, and ZIP code
  • Details about your gutter, roofing edge, soffit, fascia, or drainage needs
  • Photos or images of your home, gutters, roofline, fascia, soffits, downspouts, or related property areas
  • Preferred appointment times
  • Communication preferences
  • Any other information you choose to provide through our website forms, phone calls, SMS messages, emails, or other communication channels

We may also collect limited business or property-related information necessary to provide an estimate, prepare for a service visit, or communicate with you about your project.

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically through cookies, analytics tools, server logs, or similar technologies.

This information may include:

This information helps us understand how visitors use our website, improve performance, troubleshoot technical issues, and improve our marketing and customer experience.

We may use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To prevent fraud, misuse, unauthorized activity, or security incidents

We may also use your information to send service-related messages, such as appointment confirmations, estimate follow-ups, scheduling updates, or customer care communications.
