Soffit & Fascia FAQs

How do I know if my soffit or fascia needs to be replaced?

Soft wood, peeling paint, loose panels, stains, pest activity, or gutters pulling away are common warning signs.

What materials do you use for soffit and fascia?

Material options vary by home and scope. We commonly recommend durable aluminum or vinyl finishes selected to match the existing exterior.

Do you offer vented soffit options?

Yes. We review the roof assembly and recommend vented soffit where it supports proper attic airflow.

How long does installation usually take?

Many residential projects are completed in one to three days, depending on access, damage, and total roofline length.

Soffit & Fascia FAQs

How much does fascia and soffit replacement cost?

Fascia replacement starts at $20 per linear foot. Soffit panel installation starts at $25 per linear foot. When both are needed, the combined starting point is $45 per linear foot.

Do I need fascia repair before new gutters?

If the fascia is soft, rotten or no longer holds fasteners properly, it should be addressed before installing new gutters.

Can fascia and gutters be done together?

Yes. It often makes sense to correct the roof edge before installing or replacing gutters.

What affects fascia and soffit pricing?

Damage level, linear feet, material condition, access and ventilation needs can all affect the final price.
